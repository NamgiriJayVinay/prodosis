Success Stories

SOA Integration

The customer decided to architect a multi-tenant cloud ecosystem to offer services to major entertainment industry companies. We developed a multi-tenant ecosystem using 13 different software vendor products. This system allows the media companies to purchase the offered services as subscription services.

A Smart Shopping Cart

An electronics retailer was able to give its customers an enhanced experience plus gain better connectivity with its brick & mortar stores. The client wanted to give their customers a more unified user experience to help with device compatibility and offer a way to make secure payments. A smart shopping cart was developed using an Android-based application. After an in-depth analysis of our client’s business and goals, our team developed an enriched user experience design, while simultaneously improving the client’s connectivity to other offline stores.

Legacy System Modernization

The client decided to re-architect its major IT system that was written in legacy codes such as VB6 and COM+ technologies with a modern Service-oriented Architecture (SOA). The customer decided to begin SOA adoption by developing reusable business services and making them available across the company over the Enterprise Service Bus (ESB). The client had already decided on the technology that they wanted to use for building the ESB and had already started an initiative to move all the existing services. The results ended in increased productivity and cost savings and a Happy Client!
